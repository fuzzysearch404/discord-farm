🌾Discord Farm

Create your own virtual farm. Play solo, co-op with your friends or play against them. Fun, feature rich, but at same time user friendly and easy to play bot.

 

🎮 Main features:

  • 👨‍🌾 Grow large variety of different plants, trees and animals

  • 🏭 Produce different items from your harvest in your own factory

  • ⚖️ Sell your harvest and production to ever changing market

  • 📝 Complete personal missions, by selling produced items to various businesses

  • 🤝 Trade with other players in your Discord server

  • 🕵️ Steal some goods from other players farms

  • 🧪 Modify your plants in the laboratory

  • ⬆️ Upgrade your farm and purchase boosters

❗ Important commands:

Access the commands list, by typing %help in a text channel.

Start your game account, by typing %register.

❓ Getting help:

After typing %register, you are going to receive a long message with summary of first steps you should take. If you miss it, you can type %tutorial, to view it again.

To get information on specific command's usage, type %help command, where "command" is the name of the command you need help with.

📔 FAQ:

1) How do I know what items I have unlocked?

Check them with the %allitems command.

2) Why I am losing more money than I am earning?

There might be few reasons. The most common ones are:

a) When selling your items with %sell items, if the current market price is very low. Market prices are changing every hour. To check the prices, use one of these commands: %item or %market.

b) You are wasting way too much harvests. Every item has their harvest duration, that comes after growing duration. It is the time while the items can be harvested. If you miss the harvest - items get rotten and you don't get any rewards for that.

3) Why can't I plant more items in my farm?

Your farm has a limited space, where you can plant items. These spacing units are called "tiles". Upon starting the game you get 2 farm tiles. That means, that you can plant 2 different items or 2 items of the same type. However, these "tiles" are not really related to the actual item volume you will get, when harvesting your farm. You can check what volume of items you will get per tile with the %item command. If you have gems, you can upgrade your farm and get more tiles with the %upgrade farm command.

4) Can I plant items items from my last harvest again? (items from your inventory)

No sorry - not in this game. You have to use %plant command, that is going to cost you some constant amount of gold every time.
